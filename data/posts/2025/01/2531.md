### statement.en
No later than a month from now, Pokrovsk will once again become Krasnoarmiysk.  
The roads connecting the city (T-04-06 and M-30) are now within range of artillery fire.  
The delivery of ammunition and supplies to the Ukrainian Armed Forces is now carried out using light vehicles and tracked equipment at night. During the day and evening, they will be destroyed — no more deliveries.  
All concrete/asphalt roads have been cut off. Two oil refineries that supplied diesel to the Ukrainian forces in Pokrovsk were also destroyed during the day.
